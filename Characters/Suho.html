    • Biography

    Sung Suho is the main protagonist of the series, and is the Heir to the powers of the Shadow Monarch, his father Sung Jinwoo. Due to plans being in motion from outer gods, he was required to awaken his latent sealed powers to be able to fight back and defend his world from the new threats that have come to take over it, whilst also trying to figure out what happened to his mother Cha Hae-In.
