Biography

Go Gunhee was the Chairman of the Korean Hunter Association and one of the strongest National Level Hunters. Despite his age and declining health, he remained a powerful and influential figure in the world of hunters. Deeply committed to protecting humanity, he played a key role in the fight against the Monarchs.
