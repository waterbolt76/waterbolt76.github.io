    • Biography

    Sung Jinwoo is Known as the Second Shadow Monarch, and is the heir of the title that was passed down from the previous Shadow Monarch Ashborn. He was originally nicknamed the weakest hunter, but due to the Cartenon Temple where he is then given the leveling System, and begins his work on slowly getting stronger and stronger to the point that he becomes the strongest in his world, and becomes the Shadow Monarch. Then leaves and fights Outer Gods.
