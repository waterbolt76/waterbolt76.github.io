Chapter 1

Welcome to the first chapter of Solo Leveling Ragnarok. This chapter introduces the protagonist and sets the stage for the epic journey ahead.

The story kicks off some time after the events of the original Solo Leveling.

We’re introduced to Sung Su-ho, the son of the legendary hunter Sung Jin-Woo. Su-ho is living a relatively normal high school life, unaware of his father’s true legacy. However, strange phenomena begin to occur—dimensional cracks, monsters, and whispers of gates returning.

During a seemingly ordinary day, Su-ho gets caught in a mysterious incident involving a gate. Where monsters appear to have taken over some Hunters and started to slaughter the nearby area. During this time everyone is being evacuated and taken to safety, and while this is all occuring Suho sees one of his classmates surrounded by these overtaken hunters and tries to help his classmate, unbeknownst to him. He awakens his long forgotten and sealed power, and gets access to the System that his father created for him, based on a previous design. The Leveling System.

Stay tuned for more updates and enjoy the story!
